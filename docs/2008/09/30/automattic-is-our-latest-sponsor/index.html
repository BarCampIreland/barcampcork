Automattic is our latest sponsor

Sep 30, 2008
1 min read
automattic barcamp barcampcork wordpress wordpress.com

We’re genuinely blown away that Donncha, Toni, Matt and the guys n gals in Automattic are sponsoring BarCamp Cork II. You’ll probably know them better as the people behind Wordpress, Wordpress.com, Wordpress MU and Akismet.

This blog and thousands of other Irish ones, run on the software created by these guys and are protected from spam by them too. It all runs like a charm on a Blacknight shared hosting account.

Donncha will also be giving a talk which, if it’s anything like the one in Waterford, will be a must-attend.

We’ve setup a PayPal facility to make sponsorship as easy as possible. In the interests of disclosure, you should know that the sponsorship money will be held in an Argolon Solutions/LouderVoice bank account.
